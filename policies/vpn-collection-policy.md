# VPN collection policy

## Collection Policy (VPN) <a href="#docs-internal-guid-9469104b-7fff-1d0e-1dfb-a37d6c6d9343" id="docs-internal-guid-9469104b-7fff-1d0e-1dfb-a37d6c6d9343"></a>

This policy explains what limited data Wave Host collects for VPN services and why.

### What We Collect

* Basic account details required to provide service (for example: account ID, email, and billing status).
* Operational and security metadata needed to run the VPN platform (for example: connection timestamps, session duration, total bandwidth usage, server/region selected, and abuse or security events such as failed authentication attempts).
* Support history when you contact us.

### What We Do Not Collect

* We do not monitor or log the contents of your traffic.
* We do not log your browsing activity, DNS queries, or the destination IPs of your connections.
* We do not sell your VPN data to third parties.

### Why We Collect Data

* To provide and maintain VPN access.
* To troubleshoot connectivity issues you report.
* To prevent abuse, fraud, and violations of our policies.
* To respond to valid legal requests.

### Data Access and Retention

* Access is restricted to authorized staff who require it for operations, support, or security.
* Connection metadata is retained for \[e.g., 30 days] before being purged, unless a longer period is needed for an active abuse or security investigation.
* Account and billing data is retained only as long as reasonably required for service delivery, legal obligations, billing, or abuse prevention. By using our VPN service, you agree to this collection policy.&#x20;
