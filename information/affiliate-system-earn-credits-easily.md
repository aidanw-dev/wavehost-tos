# Affiliate System: Earn Credits Easily!

Our affiliate system is a simple and effective way to earn credits for your account. Here’s how it works:

1. Share your unique referral link with new customers.
2. When they place an order and it becomes active, you earn 5% of their total order as credits.
3. Use your earned credits towards future purchases!



To become a Affiliate go to the dashboard and press [Affiliates](https://billing.wavehost.org/affiliates.php)

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (6).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Press the "Activate Affiliate Account" button and your are now an Affiliate!

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (7).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**How It Works:**

Share your unique referral link with new customers and earn rewards! Once they place an order and it becomes active, you'll receive 5% of their total order as a discount.

⚠️ Important: You must provide your unique referral link to receive credit. The use of alternate accounts is strictly prohibited and will result in termination.

**Payout/Withdrawal Process:**

You can request a withdrawal once you reach the minimum required amount. Simply press the withdrawal button, and your request will be sent to our billing/sales department for review. Approval is subject to verification. Please note that payouts will be issued as credits to your account.
